CS50 Flashcards

A web application where users can create, review, and delete flashcards using a simple "spaced repetition" algorithm.

What can the app do? Along with creating, reviewing, and deleting cards, users will also be able to review their statistics on both a general and card-by-card level. Users logged in under a "teacher" account can assign cards to all student accounts, and review all students' progress.

What tools were used to make the app? The project uses Python (Flask) on the backend; Javascript, HTML, and CSS on the frontend; and an SQLite database to store the data.

How do I use it? To begin, you can simply click on "Create a New Card." Once you have created a few flashcards, you can click on "Review Cards" to review your flashcards. Finally, after you've done both of these things, you can can click on "My Stats" to see how you've performed so far.

Additional Teacher Tools: If (and only if!) you are logged in as a teacher, you can also click on the "Teacher Tools" dropdown menu. Here, you can choose to review the statistics for student accounts. You can also share cards that you have created with students.
